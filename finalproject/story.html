Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Nashville, Arkansas, Off the Beaten Track is more than an event venue — it's a story of transformation, history, and heart.

What once stood as a forgotten industrial building has been reimagined into a stunning 6,000-square-foot space designed for unforgettable celebrations. Originally built in the 1960s as a manufacturing facility for soil products, the building served many roles — from a bustling warehouse to a trucking hub — before sitting vacant for years. That all changed in 2018, when owner Gail Hearnsberger saw its potential. With a vision for restoration and a passion for breathing life back into historic spaces, she embarked on a “sow's ear to silk purse” renovation journey. When discussing the renovations with a friend, Gail described the location with a smile: “Well, it's just off the beaten track.” The name was perfect — and it stuck. Today, Off the Beaten Track is a place where rustic character meets elegant design — ideal for weddings, reunions, corporate events, and celebrations of every kind. Step off the beaten path and experience a venue as unique as your story.
